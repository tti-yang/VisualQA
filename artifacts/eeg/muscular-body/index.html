Body, head, or cable movement

What you will see

Large irregular shifts across multiple channels; abrupt baseline changes; sometimes followed by slow recovery.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Was the movement limited to a short identifiable period?
  • Did it occur during a response that is inherent to the task?
  • Does the artifact affect all conditions equally?
  • Is correction likely to remove neural activity as well?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Improve seating and cable stabilization.
  • Annotate and reject affected periods.
  • Redesign response periods when movement is unavoidable.
  • Avoid assuming that ICA can separate complex, nonstationary movements.
