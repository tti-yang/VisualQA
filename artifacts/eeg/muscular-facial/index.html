Facial, jaw, or scalp muscle activity

What you will see

High-frequency activity.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Is the activity brief or sustained?
  • Is it localized to channels outside the region of interest?
  • Is it linked to the participant’s response or experimental condition?
  • Would removing the affected channels distort the spatial analysis?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Reduce muscle tension during recording.
  • Annotate or reject contaminated segments.
  • Remove clearly identified ICA components.
  • Exclude persistently contaminated channels when justified.
