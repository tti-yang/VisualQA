ECG / heartbeat

What you will see

Repetitive waveform synchronized with the heartbeat; may be stronger near inferior or reference electrodes.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Is the artifact visible in only a few electrodes or broadly distributed?
  • Is it random relative to the experimental event?
  • Is a reliable ECG channel available for identification?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Record ECG when relevant.
  • Use ICA, SSP, or regression when the component is clearly identified.
  • Retain the data when contamination is minimal and not event-related.
