Electrode bridging

What you will see

Two or more nearby electrodes show unusually similar signals and very small electrical distance.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Are the channels merely correlated because of genuine neural activity, or are they almost electrically identical?
  • How central are the bridged electrodes to the planned spatial analysis?
  • How many electrodes are involved?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Prevent excessive gel application.
  • Identify bridged electrodes using spatial or electrical-distance measures.
  • Mark or interpolate affected channels when appropriate.
