Vertical eye movement

What you will see

Slow frontal deflection resembling a partial blink, often with opposite polarity above and below the eye.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Is the movement systematic across conditions?
  • Does it occur before or after the critical ERP interval?
  • Can it be distinguished from a blink or frontal neural activity?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Detect using vertical EOG or eye tracking.
  • Reject affected trials.
  • Probably consider correction when the pattern is stable and well characterized.
