Horizontal eye movement

What you will see

Step-like voltage shift in horizontal EOG; lateralized scalp pattern that changes with gaze direction.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • How large was the gaze shift?
  • Did participants systematically look toward one experimental condition?
  • Does the artifact overlap with the lateralized ERP window?
  • Is the EOG or eye-tracking signal sensitive enough to detect small movements?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Reject affected trials.
  • Use HEOG or eye-tracking criteria.
  • Improve fixation instructions.
  • Use correction only when its assumptions are appropriate.
