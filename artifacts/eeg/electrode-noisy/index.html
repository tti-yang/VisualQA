Noisy or unstable channel

What you will see

Excessive variance, persistent high-frequency noise, repeated bursts, or activity much larger than neighboring electrodes.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Is the problem continuous or limited to certain periods?
  • Is it clearly different from genuine local neural activity?
  • Would excluding the channel remove an important electrode from the analysis?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Repair during acquisition.
  • Mark bad time periods or the entire channel.
  • Exclude before ICA when appropriate.
  • Interpolate only when justified.
