Sweat

What you will see

Very slow, large-amplitude drift or changing baseline; often affects several nearby electrodes.

Judgment calls and decision-making considerations

  • Is the drift local or widespread?
  • Does it occur gradually or only during a short period?
  • Can the affected segment be removed without losing a large part of the experiment?
  • Would high-pass filtering distort the ERP of interest?

Possible solutions & responses

  • Improve room temperature and participant comfort.
  • Inspect and repair electrode contacts.
  • Reject severely affected segments.
  • Use filtering cautiously.
